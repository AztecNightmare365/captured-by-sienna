Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Captured by Sienna website (“we,” “us,” the “Site”) handles information when you visit. Captured by Sienna is an independent photography service based in Marion, New York.

1. Information we collect directly

The Site itself does not have accounts, contact forms, or trackers that we operate. We do not directly collect names, emails, or payment details through this website.

2. Third-party services

To show our work and let you book, the Site embeds tools run by other companies. When you interact with them, those companies — not us — collect and process your data under their own privacy policies:

  • Instagram / Meta — our portfolio displays embedded Instagram posts. Meta may set cookies and collect usage data when these load. See Meta’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google (Appointment Scheduling) — booking is handled by Google Calendar. When you book, you provide your name, email, and requested time directly to Google, which shares it with the photographer to fulfill your booking. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — the Site is hosted by GitHub, which may log basic request data (such as IP address) for security. See GitHub’s Privacy Statement.

3. How booking information is used

If you book a session, the contact details you submit are used only to schedule, confirm, and carry out your photography session. We do not sell your information.

4. Cookies

We do not set our own cookies. Embedded Instagram and Google content may set third-party cookies on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Children’s privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information. Minors photographed for a session are booked and consented to by a parent or guardian.

6. Your choices

To ask what booking information we hold about you, or to request its correction or deletion, contact us using the email listed on our homepage.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted here.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at the email on our homepage.
